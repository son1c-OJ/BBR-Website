BattleBit Remastered

BattleBit: Remastered (BBR) is a Unity-based online multiplayer FPS, created with low-poly models and realistic mechanics. It combines elements from casual and hardcore shooters with minimalistic graphics to facilitate maximum playability even on older machines.
